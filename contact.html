When I began attending yoga class with Nicole D’Andrea 6 months ago, I had hoped to regain some much-needed flexibility in my body. What I got was so much more than that.

Nicole welcomes and acknowledges her students in her warm way. Breathing deeply, we are encouraged to think about our intention for our practice that day. While learning the movements, Nicole tells a little about the philosophy of yoga, the why behind what we are doing.

There you are, making progress with Nicole helping in her knowledgeable way. She is on the floor showing us the poses, helping with form, and yes, cheerleading.

As a result of coming to class with Nicole, I am not only more flexible in body, but even more so in spirit. If you want to clear your mind and relax your body and your being, to be challenged in a safe and healthy way, to overcome some old ways of thinking, to feel five or ten years younger in a few weeks, come and see for yourself. You won’t regret it.

-Eve C.
